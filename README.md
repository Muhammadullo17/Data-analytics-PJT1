# 🎯 Project Objectives

✅ Design an online platform where customers can easily order food
✅ Improve restaurant order management and workflow
✅ Reduce errors in manual ordering systems
✅ Provide managers with sales tracking and reporting features

---

# ⚠️ Problem Statement

**Customer Problem:**
Customers may experience difficulty ordering food when menus are not easily accessible or when orders must be placed manually.

**Restaurant Problem:**
Restaurants that rely on manual order processing may face delays, mistakes, and inefficient communication between staff.

**Management Problem:**
Managers may lack proper tools to monitor sales, track orders, and manage menu items efficiently.

---

# 🔧 System Features & Scope

### For Customers

🔐 User registration and login
📋 View restaurant menu and food items
🛒 Place food orders online
📍 Track order status
💳 Online payment option

---

### For Restaurant Staff

📥 Receive and process incoming orders
🔄 Update order status (Preparing, Ready, Delivered)
📊 Manage daily order activities

---

### For Managers

📋 Add, edit, or remove menu items
📊 Monitor sales and order statistics
📈 View reports about restaurant performance
⚙️ Manage system users and operations
